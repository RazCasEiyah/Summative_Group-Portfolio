What is an OC? And what is OC Art?

An OC (Original Character) is a character you made or sketched yourself, not from an existing show, game, or story. With its own personality, story, and design, they are often used to express creativity, explore new ideas, or build original worlds.


Step into a tapestry of creativity, where original characters spring to life through the eyes of our group of artists and storytellers. Each piece pulses with its own spirit, personality, and flair, weaving our individual visions ina vibrant, shared universe.


From delicate sketches to bold digital paintings, from whimiscal concept art to fully realized illustrations, our work is a celebration of imagination, collaboration, and the magic of storytelling through character design. Together, we invite you to explore, dream, and wander through the worlds we've shaped.
