Andeng

i first created my oc when i was in 4th grade when i first listened to vocaloid and was introduced to fandoms from other shows. Back then i make oc’s who are magical as any typical young artist first started drawing. But as i reached whighschool, i decided for my oc’s to be more of the ‘mulan’ or ‘kocho shinobu’ aspects, a baby faced girl who dresses like a man and almost mistaken as a man, uses any tricks to defeat her enemies, one of my example is Akuma Hotarou, she is a normal bystander in the morning, but she has a vigilante group made out of girls, to get rid of criminals at night, and her underlings are girls she picks up from the streets and raised like a caretaker..some became her spies and some fights with her (this was the time i was into Tokyo Revengers a lot).
